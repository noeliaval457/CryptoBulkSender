### FAQ — Crypto Transfer Automation Tool

Q: What is Crypto Transfer Automation Tool?
A: It is a secure and powerful multi-chain automation tool that allows you to send cryptocurrencies (batch or single transfers), schedule recurring payments, and automate treasury operations across Solana, Ethereum, Tron, BSC, and other blockchains.

Q: Which blockchains are supported?
A: Currently supported networks include:Solana
Ethereum + EVM chains (BSC, Polygon, Avalanche, etc.). More networks are planned for future updates.

Q: Is this bot safe to use?
A: Yes. The tool is designed with strong security practices: private keys never leave your device, transactions are simulated before execution, and sensitive data is encrypted.Features & Functionality

Q: Can I send to multiple addresses at once?
A: Yes. The bot supports batch transfers — you can send crypto to hundreds or thousands of addresses in one operation using CSV or Excel files.

Q: Does it support scheduled and recurring transfers?
A: Yes. You can schedule one-time or recurring transfers (daily, weekly, monthly) using cron-style scheduling.

Q: Does the bot optimize transaction fees?
A: Yes. It automatically calculates optimal gas/priority fees and lets you choose between economy, standard, or fast modes.

Q: Can I track my transfers?
A: Absolutely. The bot provides real-time transaction status updates and maintains a complete transfer history with export options.

Q: Do I need to share my private keys?
A: No. Your private keys stay locally on your machine. The bot never sends them anywhere.

Q: How are private keys protected?
A: Private keys are stored locally and can be optionally encrypted with a master password. They are never logged or transmitted.

Q: What happens if the bot crashes during a batch transfer?
A: The bot keeps detailed logs and a transfer queue. You can resume interrupted batches safely.

Q: Is this tool legal?
A: Yes, as long as you use it for legitimate purposes (personal transfers, business payments, airdrops, treasury management, etc.). Always comply with local laws and tax regulations.Performance & Troubleshooting

Tron: under 3 seconds  
Ethereum: 15–60 seconds (depending on network congestion)
